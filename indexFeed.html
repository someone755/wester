A Holiday For Everyone

Wester is a tradition started by two goofballs in 2016. It celebrates waffles, friendship, love, and more waffles. It is celebrated every year on the Friday after Nutella day; This is why waffles with Nutella are especially encouraged. In 2016 the holiday was on February 12th, and February 10th in 2017. The 2018 celebration is scheduled for February 9th.

The Story

In late 2015, two friends debated religion and the Easter holiday. Coming to a conclusion that their beliefs could not ever come between them, they had an idea: A holiday that everybody could celebrate, regardless of their nationality, religion, health, or other human trait.

In German, the Wester holiday, translated as 'Waffelaufstand', is a transformation of the German word for 'armed uprising' into something waffle-relatedâ€”something no one man could hate. As a parallel to Easter, the rough translation of 'Waffelaufstand' into English is "resurrection of the waffle," yet another nod to the newfound peace- and friendship-inspiring tradition.
